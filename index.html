About the Studio

Triumphant Development Group is an indie studio led by James Young and MistaNastyTime904 out of Jacksonville, Florida. We focus on realism, grounded systems, and heartfelt narratives. Our games are built in Unreal Engine 5.6 with attention to Florida’s ecosystems and culture.

Studio Pillars

  • Immersive realism without losing fun.
  • Player‑driven stories and meaningful choices.
  • Transparent development and community collaboration.
